Hello! My name is Nelson Gutierrez Jose, but you can call me N.G. Jose or whatever you prefer.

I began my journey when my Biology teacher asked me to create a short film about the circulatory system of reptiles. She loved it, which inspired me to make a few more films, including El Viaje Interior, La Noche del Silencio, and Entre Sombras.

Currently, I’ve decided to focus on random comedy animations. I'm the creator of Syobon Action: New Something, Madre Molesta, PixelParley, and La Familia Usuario. I tend to take a long time on my projects, so I appreciate your patience!

While my animation and art style may not be perfect, I embrace it. However, I’m looking forward to developing a different style in the future.
